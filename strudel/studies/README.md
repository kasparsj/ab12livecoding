# Strudel Song-Structure Studies

These studies extract reusable structure from public Strudel examples. They do
not copy full third-party songs; they link to the original source and describe
the arrangement logic in reusable terms.

Public source availability is not a reuse grant. Several examples are
unlicensed repositories, covers, or studies of credited third-party
compositions, so these notes should be treated as analysis-only unless a source
license clearly allows more. Studies 09 and 10 use an AI-authored repository as
supplemental structure evidence and are marked accordingly.

## Selection Criteria

Accepted structures show at least one of these:

- Clear section contrast.
- Staged layer entry or exit.
- Explicit transition logic.
- Performance controls or live toggles.
- A reusable variation system.
- Distinct musical roles such as melody, harmony, bass, drums, texture, and effects.

Rejected examples were usually too short, too fragmentary, too tool-focused, or
too close to a single static loop.
